# Sync — Meaningful Networking at Tech Events

Sync helps people at tech events move beyond surface-level small talk and build genuine connections. It matches attendees intelligently, gives them conversation starters, and surfaces who actually learned something from whom.

---

## The Problem

Tech events are packed with interesting people, but most conversations stay shallow. You exchange names, hand over a business card, and never follow up. Time is limited, the schedule is dense, and it's hard to know who to talk to or what to talk about.

Sync solves this by making the right introductions at the right time — and giving the conversation somewhere to go.

---

## How It Works

### Mode 1 — Random Connect

For when you want to meet someone new, no context needed.

1. **Set yourself as Idle** when you're free and open to a conversation.
2. **Get matched** with another idle attendee. The app shows you hints — their role, interests, what they're working on — so you can find each other in the room.
3. **Meet in person.** Both of you tap "We found each other" to mark yourselves as Engaged.
4. **Get a conversation prompt** to kick things off — a question or topic tailored to help you share experiences.
5. **After the conversation**, each person submits:
   - What they learned from the other person
   - A justification for why that learning stood out
6. **The other person reviews** what you wrote and rates your understanding on a scale of 1–10, marking whether you got it right.
7. **Mark the conversation** as Meaningful or Casual. Only meaningful conversations count toward your score.

---

### Mode 2 — Interest-Based Swipe Connect

For when you want to find people who think like you do.

1. **Browse the event schedule** — talks, speakers, topics.
2. **Rate each talk**: Interested, Not Sure, or Not Interested.
3. **During a talk you're skipping**, the app shows you other attendees who are also free right now — people who rated the same talk as Not Interested.
4. **Swipe right** on people you want to connect with, **swipe left** to pass.
5. **Mutual right swipe = match.** You both get a conversation prompt and can find each other.
6. The same post-conversation learning and rating loop applies.

---

## After the Conversation

### Learning Exchange

Both participants write up what they took away from the conversation about the other person — their perspective, their experience, their insight. The other person reads it and rates how well you understood them.

This creates accountability. You're not just chatting — you're listening.

### Meaningful vs Casual

At the end, you decide: was this a meaningful conversation or just casual? Meaningful conversations feed into the leaderboard. Casual ones don't.

---

## Leaderboard

A public ranking of attendees by how well others have understood them across meaningful conversations. High scores mean people genuinely walk away learning something from you.

It rewards depth over volume.

---

## Conversation Initiator

Every match comes with a prompt — a question or scenario designed to get beyond "what do you do?" and into real experience sharing. Prompts rotate and are tuned to surface opinions, stories, and lessons rather than job titles.

---

## User States

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Idle | Available and open to a match |
| Engaged | In an active conversation |

Your state is visible to the matching system. You control it.
